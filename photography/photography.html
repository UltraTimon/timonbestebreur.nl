My snapshots of the beautiful world around me

I'm not that much of a photographer. I don't have a profile picture of myself holding some over-the-top camera. I don't carry a camera with me all day (not counting the 3.5 MP camera on my Nokia 3310). I don't know anything about composition, aperture, exposure etcetera.* But, I am fascinated with the world around me, and find that there is lots of beauty to be found all around me. Thus, I try to document these things using a classic retro point-and-shoot camera (which takes suprisingly good pictures by the way) to share some of the aesthetically pleasing sights around me. I have one simple rule: No zooming allowed. Everything you see is photographed exactly as I saw it. Enjoy!

Take a look at my series of photographs on flowers, the city centre of Delft or the windmills at kinderdijk.

*Fun fact: I wanted to give at least three examples, as that looks stylistically more satisfuyuing to me, yet I could only come up with two. I had to search DuckDuckGo for a third one :) Luckily there are nice websites like this one that provide all the information you might need on photography. Thank you internet!
