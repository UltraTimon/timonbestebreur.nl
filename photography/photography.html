My snapshots of the beautiful world around me

I'm not that much of a photographer. I don't have a profile picture of myself holding some over-the-top camera. I don't carry a camera with me all day (not counting the 3.5 MP camera on my Nokia 3310). I don't know anything about composition, aperture,s exposure etcetera.* But, I am fascinated with the world around me, and find that there is lots of beauty to be found all around me. Thus, I try to document these things using a classic retro point-and-shoot camera (which takes suprisingly good pictures by the way) to share some of the aesthetically pleasing sights around me. I have one simple rule: No zooming allowed. Everything you see is photographed exactly as I saw it. Enjoy!
