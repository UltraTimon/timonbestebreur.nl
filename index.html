The juicy details!

Hi there, I'm Timon. You might be wondering where you ended up or what this website is all about. Well, most of the personal websites around are the most boring collections of html files around. To make everyone's life a bit more fun, I tried to spice up my '... and here you can find my resume... ' game a little by inserting some sarcasm. So, if you were wondering: Yes, the 'quick links' section contains lots of jokes. I do not think my plants are shitty. I love my plants!

I have a very positive self-image, I have high levels of self-confidence and I'm very proud of my blog, podcast, photos, micro-garden, woodcarvings, music, recipes, resume and collection of lessons, since these cover a quite wide area of skill and expertise. The fun thing about it is that you can undoubtably find someone else that does any of these things better than me. But all of them, together? I've yet to find someone. Throw in the fact that I'm building a Freaking SATELLITE this year and we've got a pretty epic combination of skills.

I do all of these things in order to become broadly skilled instead of an expert in a narrow field. The goal is to become a polymath or renaissance man. So far I really like the variety and creativity that this path has unleashed in me. This website is partly a documentation of this pursuit.

Feel free to click around. I tried to make each page interesting, so there's a lot to see. If you'll excuse me? A kind lady from across the street bought too many lemons and offered some to me. See you later!
